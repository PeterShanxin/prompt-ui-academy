# Community Code of Conduct

We want Prompt UI Academy to be a welcoming place for learners, designers, developers, educators, and first-time contributors.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- being respectful, patient, and constructive;
- explaining unfamiliar terminology without gatekeeping;
- giving feedback about the work rather than the person;
- acknowledging different languages, abilities, and experience levels;
- accepting correction and taking responsibility for mistakes.

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, and publishing another person's private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to repository owner [@PeterShanxin](https://github.com/PeterShanxin). Reports will be reviewed fairly and kept confidential where possible. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and other spaces where someone represents this project.
